Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2025

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

The Lightning Role Discord bot collects and stores the following information:

We do not share your data with third parties. The data is stored securely and is used solely to provide the bot's functionality.

If you have questions about your data or would like it removed, please contact us at contato@bernacripto.com.br.
